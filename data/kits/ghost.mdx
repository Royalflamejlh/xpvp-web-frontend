## Active

- **Ghost Mode**: Left-click with the Soul Lantern to go invisible and invincible for 15 seconds. During this time, you cannot interact with anything or use any items. Cooldown: 1 minute 30 seconds.

## Passive

- **Hidden Nametag**: Your nametag cannot be seen through walls, allowing you to remain hidden from other players.

## Items

- **Soul Lantern** named **Lantern of Christmas Past**
- **Compass**
