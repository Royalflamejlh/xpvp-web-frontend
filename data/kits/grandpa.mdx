## Active

- **Grandpa's Cane**: Hit a player with your cane to launch them into the air. There is no cooldown, allowing you to continuously knock enemies back.

## Passive

- **Crouch for Strength**: Gain increased strength while crouching, allowing for more powerful attacks when you are in a defensive stance.

## Items

- **Stick** named **Grandpa's Cane**
- **Compass**
