## Active

- **Hunter Vision**: Left-click with the bow to reveal the outlines of nearby players within 50 blocks for 20 seconds. Cooldown: 1 minute 30 seconds.

## Passive

- **Hunter's Shot**: Arrows slow the target and cause them to bleed for 3 seconds. Each arrow deals 1 true damage in addition to normal damage.
