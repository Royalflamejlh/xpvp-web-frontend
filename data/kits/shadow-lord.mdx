tags: ['Stealth', 'Disabler']

---

## Active

- **Shadow Realm**: Hit a player with the Shadow Cleaver to take them into the shadow realm for 30 seconds. Cooldown: 5 minutes.

## Passive

- **Darkness Buff**: Gain resistance and speed when in low light level areas.

## Items

- **Compass**
- **Netherite Hoe** named **Shadow Cleaver**
