## Active

- **Chronowatch Rewind**: Use the Chronowatch to set a location, then click again to rewind time and return to that saved location. The ability lasts for 30 seconds, allowing you to travel back. Cooldown: 2 minutes.

## Passive

- **Chronomancer's Vision**: Gain the ability to see the past locations of other players, offering insight into their previous movements and positioning.
