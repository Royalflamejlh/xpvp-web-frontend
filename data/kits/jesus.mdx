## Active

- **Click to Heal**: Use the Totem of Undying to heal entities (players or mobs) when clicked. Cooldown: 4 seconds.
  - Heals the target entity instantly when used.

## Passive

- **Walk on Water**: Allows you to walk on water without sinking, providing mobility across bodies of water.

## Items

- **Totem of Undying** named **Easter**
- **Compass**
