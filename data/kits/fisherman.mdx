---

## Active

- **Hook and Pull**: Use the fishing rod to hook and pull players towards you. There is no cooldown for this ability, allowing for repeated use.

## Passive

- **Fish for Mushrooms**: Fish faster and have a chance to catch up to 5 mushrooms at once, which can be used to make soup for healing.

## Items

- **Fishing Rod** named **The Codfather**
- **Compass**
