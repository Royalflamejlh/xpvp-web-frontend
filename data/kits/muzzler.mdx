---
title: 'Muzzler'
tags: ['Disabler', 'Support']
summary: 'Uses silencing abilities to prevent enemies from using their powers. Can silence nearby players or attackers for a limited time.'
---

## Active

- **Silence Nearby Players**: Right-click with the Muzzle to stop all nearby players' abilities within 30 blocks for 1 minute.

## Passive

- **Silence Attacker**: Players who attack the Muzzler lose their abilities for 10 seconds.

## Items

- **Paper** named **Muzzle**
- **Compass**
