## Active

- **Double Jump**: Press space twice to double jump with a 20-second cooldown. Amplifies jump height by 1.3x.
- **Stomper's Fall**: Fall damage is transferred to nearby entities upon landing. Maximum fall damage is 10, with up to 2 true damage applied to enemies.

## Passive

- Fall damage is transferred to entities within a certain radius when landing.
- Damage multiplier of 1x for regular fall damage transfer.

## Items

- **Compass**
- **Leather Boots** named **Black Airforce 1s**
