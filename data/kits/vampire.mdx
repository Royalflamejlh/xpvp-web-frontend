## Active

- **Bat Transform**: Click with the Bat Crystal to transform into a bat for 30 seconds, allowing for quick mobility and evasion. Cooldown: 3 minutes.

## Passive

- **Vampire Damage**: Heal 20% of the damage you deal to enemies, allowing the Vampire to sustain itself in combat.

## Items

- **Echo Shard** named **Bat Crystal**
- **Compass**
