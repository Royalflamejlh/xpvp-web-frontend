## Active

- **Madman's Beef Buff**: Eating beef will reduce damage taken for 7 seconds. Cooldown: 20 seconds.

## Passive

- **Weaken Nearby Enemies**: Players within a 20 block radius take increased damage, making the Madman a dangerous presence in close proximity.
- **Regenerate Beef**: Automatically regenerate beef over time, ensuring a consistent supply for damage reduction.
