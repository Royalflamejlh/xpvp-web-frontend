## Active

- **Bee Attack**: Hit someone with the Honeycomb to send a swarm of bees after them. The bees will target and deal damage to the enemy. No cooldown.

## Passive

- **Generate Bees**: You slowly generate pet bees that will follow you and can be used to attack enemies. You can have a maximum of 10 bees at any given time, with a new bee generated every 45 seconds.

## Items

- **Compass**
- **Honeycomb** named **Honeycomb**
