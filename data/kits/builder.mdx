## Active

- **Builder's Brick Trap**: Attack with a brick to trap other players, immobilizing them temporarily. Cooldown: 30 seconds.

## Passive

- **Regenerate Bricks**: Continuously regenerates bricks over time, with a new brick generated every 2 seconds, allowing for constant trapping.
